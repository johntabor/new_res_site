Rutgers Entrepreneurial Society

The premier innovation community at Rutgers University - New Brunswick. We are a community of creators, inventors, and entrepreneurs passionate about student entrepreneurship.

About RES

RES is the primary hub of student innovation at Rutgers University - New Brunswick. Our membership spans all academic disciplines from finance to computer science to english and everything in between. Our network is composed of active student organizations and dedicated faculty across Rutgers' undergraduate schools -- all dedicated to providing support and resources to anyone who wants to build an independent project or business here at Rutgers.



Our Events

RES is passionate about providing students with opportunities to learn new skills and connect with entrepreneurs. We host a variety of events through each semester which provide a lot of value to students.

Speaker Series

Throughout the semester, RES has entrepreneurs and innovators come in and speak. We’ve had CEO’s of billion-dollar tech companies. We’ve had co-founders of start-ups that were acquired for millions of dollars. We’ve had graphic designers, businesspeople, artists, engineers, and many more. We firmly believe that when creative student thinkers and experienced innovators come together, the potential benefits are limitless.

Workshops

We hold workshops in which talented entrepreneurs and or student innovators teach new knowledge and directly interface with the audience. These events are more interactive and attendees leave with new, useful knowledge.

RISE

RISE is the weekend-long staple conference of our organization. Each semester we host a rigorous, 36-hr competition, where students create and pitch a start-up from scratch. Packed into a this weekend are incredible keynote speakers. skilled student workshops, and students from nearby universities in attendance.
